Photos, times and coordinates

You can find more information about times of taking photos by Lisanne and Kris on the Cold Case blog. Not all of their photos are known in public. Here is a composition of almost all known photos taken on April 1st 2014 and some information when they were taken. I numbered the photos from 1 to 17 so that it would be better to refer to them.
