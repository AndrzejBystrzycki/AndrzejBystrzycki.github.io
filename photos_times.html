Conclusions:

1. The most exact result is probably the result for photo 2 (Kris on the bridge). It confirms that the girls started their hike at about 11:00 am. It is possible to determine the time of taking photo 2 with an accuracy of 5 minutes (see here).

2. Photo 1 (486) may have been taken the day before. That means that the girls may have been on the El Pianista trail between 12:00 and 13:00 on March 31st.

3. There is a problem with assigning photos of ravines to EXIF times. There seems to be only 2 times that would match the places where the 3 photos were taken. See point "Photos of ravines" how I tried to identify the places.
