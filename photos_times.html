Conclusions:

1. The most exact result is probably the result for photo 2 (Kris on the bridge). It confirms that the girls started their hike at about 11:00 am.

2. Photo 500 is probably a fake photo.

3. Photo 1 (482) may have been taken the day before. That means that the girls may have been on the El Pianista trail between 12:00 and 13:00 on March 31st.
