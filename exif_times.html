There is also an English version of the list in the excellent article. I prefer the French version because it seems to be more accurate. Photo numbers are numbers of original images from file names in the camera. "Ordinal number" is a subsequent number of a photo used in my text. For example I refer to photo number 486 as photo 1 in my text. Photos not known in public are denoted "???" in the column "photo". Corrected EXIF time is obtained by subtracting 6 hours from the original EXIF time.
