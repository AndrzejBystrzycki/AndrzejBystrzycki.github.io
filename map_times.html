The map below is based on the information from the wikiloc website.

Each point corresponds to a photo taken at the place denoted by the point. The points denoted red are the points (a number of a photo and the time of taking the photo) indicating the way up the trail. The points denoted blue are the points indicating the way down the trail. I placed a link to the map at the top ("The map and links to photos", see above) where you can find links to the individual photos on the wikiloc website.

The times are based on the EXIF times of photos on the wikiloc website. The photos on the website were taken with two different cameras. EXIF times differ much. For example this is the CreateDate field for the two following photos: 20927635Master.jpg 2019:01:25 16:53:36
20926949Master.jpg 2019:01:25 09:40:51
Both photos were taken at the same place. I subtracted 7 hours 12 minutes 25 seconds for all photos with times after 16:00. In this way I obtained synchronized times for all photos.
